Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular dish made from pre-cooked rice that is stir-fried in a wok or frying pan with ingredients such as vegetables, vinegar, and soy sauce. It is often seasoned with soy sauce and other spices.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetable
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add vegetables, cooking until tender.
  3. Add the cooled rice and soy sauce, mixing everything together.
  4. Stir-fry for a few minutes until heated through.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with green onions if desired.
