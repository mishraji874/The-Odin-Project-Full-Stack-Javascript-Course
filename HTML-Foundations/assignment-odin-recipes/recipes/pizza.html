Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
