Noodles

Description

Noodles are a staple food in many cultures, made from unleavened dough that is rolled flat and cut into various shapes. They can be served in soups, stir-fries, or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  3. Drain the noodles and rinse with cold water.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, oil, and vinegar to taste.
  5. Serve hot or cold as desired.
